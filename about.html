About Us

Welcome to Kondaji Chivda

Since 1985, Kondaji Chivda has been a trusted name in Nashik, Maharashtra, delivering authentic Indian snacks crafted with passion and tradition. Founded by Ramesh Kondaji, our journey began with a vision to bring the rich flavors of Nashik Chivda to every household, preserving the essence of time-honored recipes.

We specialize in a variety of chivda and namkeen, including our signature Nashik Chivda, Jain Chivda, Makhnal Chivda, and more. Each product is made with handpicked, natural ingredients, ensuring premium quality and authentic taste. Our commitment to excellence extends to Jain-friendly options, catering to diverse dietary preferences without compromising flavor.

At Kondaji Chivda, we blend traditional craftsmanship with modern convenience, offering easy online ordering and exceptional customer support. Our mission is to spread joy through our delicious snacks, connecting people with the culinary heritage of India.
