About Us

Welcome to Kondaji Chivda

Established in 1985, Kondaji Chivda brings you the authentic taste of Nashik's finest snacks. Our journey began with a passion for crafting traditional Indian namkeen that delights every palate.

Using time-honored recipes and the freshest ingredients, we create chivda and snacks that embody the rich culinary heritage of India. From our signature Nashik Chivda to Jain-friendly options, we cater to all tastes.
